Rules

1. Chase is a two player game.

2. Player One uses the WASD keys.

3. Player Two uses the arrow keys.

4. Player One is trying to get his/her character to touch touch the target in the middle.

5. Player Two is trying to get his/her character to intercept Player One before they reach the target.

6. There are four differents "traps" that appear on the screen. These traps change locations every 10 seconds.

7. The red trap stops your character. The green trap speeds your character up. The yellow trap slows your character down. The purple trap reverses your controls.
